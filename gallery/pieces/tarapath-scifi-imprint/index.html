Our worldview
Science fiction is not an escape from history. It is where history goes to become strange enough to see.

Tarapath is a small independent imprint for literary science fiction rooted in South Asia: its languages, ecologies, migrations, technologies, faiths and unfinished histories.

We publish slowly—four books each year—so each one receives close editorial attention, a considered physical form and time to find its readers.

We are interested in the cosmic without losing sight of the street; in speculation as a way of thinking, not a shelf label; in futures that do not begin elsewhere.

Signals, rarely.

A seasonal letter: new books, translator conversations, reading lists and dispatches from the edge of the map. Four editions a year.
