Who runs this

The station is maintained by the Manang porters' cooperative with the DHM. Solar-powered, yak-proof enclosure, serviced each October.

Reading the verdict

“Crossable” weighs wind, wind-chill, and visibility against the slowest party on the trail that morning — not against a fit guide. When in doubt, it says so.

Get it by radio

Broadcast at 05:00 and 05:30 on 144.425 MHz from Manang. Lodge owners repeat it at breakfast.
