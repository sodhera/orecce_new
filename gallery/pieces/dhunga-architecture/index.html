04 / Practice

Two people. One table. Close attention.

Dhunga was founded in Patan in 2019 by architects Nima Shrestha and Aarya Maharjan.

We work at the scale of the room and the settlement: houses, places of work, and careful adaptations of existing buildings.

Our process begins with measured drawings, material samples, and long conversations with the people who will build and inhabit the work.
