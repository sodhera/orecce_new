The road into Manang

How to reach our door.

The journey is part jeep road, part high-mountain weather. We recommend coming slowly.

Send your jeep number by WhatsApp to +977 9846 218 730. Nima will meet you by Manang’s west chorten if you arrive after dusk.

Kathmandu to Besisahar

Tourist bus from Gongabu New Bus Park. Buy a ticket one day ahead in peak season.

6–7 hours

Besisahar to Chame

Shared 4×4 from the jeep stand near the bus park. Expect rough road and stops for tea.

7–9 hours

Chame to Upper Pisang

Walk through pine forest via Bhratang and Dhukur Pokhari. A good first acclimatisation day.

5–6 hours

Upper Pisang to Manang

Take the high trail through Ghyaru and Ngawal for the views, or the lower trail when weather closes in.

6–8 hours
