03 · The way in

The road is part of it.

Manang has no airport. Most guests arrive overland from Kathmandu, then walk the final approach for better acclimatisation. We can reserve your jeep seat with 48 hours’ notice.

Day 0 · 1,400 m

Kathmandu

Tourist bus from Gongabu at 06:30. Buy the Besisahar ticket one day ahead.

Day 1 · 760 m

Besisahar

Arrive around 14:00. Shared 4×4 jeeps leave from the north stand.

Day 1 · 2,670 m

Chame

Sleep here. Do not continue to Manang the same day; the altitude gain is too fast.

Day 3 · 3,540 m

Tashi Ghar

Walk via Upper Pisang and Ngawal. Find our red gate, 80 m east of the monastery.

Recommended · 3 days from Besisahar · 26 km walking
04 · Your hosts
We can tell when the pass will close by watching the cloud sit on Gangapurna.

Tashi and Pemba built the first four rooms beside their family home in 1987. Their daughters, Sonam and Nima, now run the lodge, still using vegetables from the courtyard greenhouse and buckwheat from Braga.

— Sonam Gurung, second generation host
